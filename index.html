From The Principal’s Desk

Schooling is on the verge of transition in current time where the nourished goal of schools is to shape young minds and create congenial atmosphere instead of acquiring mammoth degree and towering personality in the society. Now a days, everyone is thinking to shift from ‘planning percentile success’ to ‘planning pursuit of excellence’ in whatever we aspire to do. The term ‘Schooling’ has become the buzzword and has also become the mantra of success for the young brains who are striving towards achieving dream with never seen before with such utmost effort. In this day and age, it is given the assorted atmosphere of the classroom, the need of the time is not catering to the entire class as one sample group but reach out to each individual through differentiated instruction method so that each child can receive and work towards achieving excellence as per their innate strengths and abilities.
